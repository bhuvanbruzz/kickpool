What is Quick Ride - Carpool & Bikepool

We all like going together. Quick Ride will help you to connect with other like-minded professionals who are traveling on the same route and same time. Quick Ride helps commuters to start sharing the ride instead of traveling alone. Innovative technology to discover, connect, route match, coordinate and cost-share in a seamless manner. Quick Ride automates end to end process of carpooling & bikepooling and makes pooling safe, comfortable and affordable for the urban commuters
